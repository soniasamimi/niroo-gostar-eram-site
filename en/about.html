Our history

Founded in Bushehr, built on technical breadth

Niroo Gostar Eram was officially registered as a private joint-stock company on 11 Esfand 1387 (March 2009), under registration number 7456, with its principal office in Bushehr.

From the outset, the company's scope was built around the systems industrial and commercial sites rely on every day: electrical power, automation and control, and mechanical infrastructure. Over the years, this has translated into hands-on project work across installation, maintenance, and engineering consultation.

Today, the company focuses on three core service lines — electrical installation and maintenance, industrial automation and PLC systems, and mechanical & HVAC systems — serving industrial, commercial, and residential clients.
